COVID-19

The global pandemic.

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). It was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, Hubei, China, and has resulted in an ongoing pandemic. The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the COVID‑19 outbreak a pandemic on 11 March 2020. The United Nations (UN) and WHO have praised India's response to the pandemic as 'comprehensive and robust,' terming the lockdown restrictions as 'aggressive but vital' for containing the spread and building necessary healthcare infrastructure.

PM Care Fund

National fund by the people, for the people.

A public charitable trust fund set up to strength the battle against COVID-19. Aimed with the mission to support and provide assistance to public healthcare in kind of emergency, calamity or distress. Provides funding to relevant research and creation or upgradation of healthcare and pharmaceutical companies. PM-CARES fund is said to solely and entirely consist voluntary contributions from any individual(s) and/or organization(s), and does not receive budgetary support to further the cause.

The Effect of COVID-19

Struggles of the nation.

India's economy has witnessed massive shock due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The livelihoods and wage-earnings of low-income families have been impacted and many people face unemployment challenges. Ensuring adquate supply of food and essentials has been a challange in this time of crisis. Thankfully there are many NGO'S and organizations setup to help the country's poor become self-sufficient and better prepared.

Flatten The Curve

What is it and how can we tackle it?

In epidemiology, the idea of slowing a virus' spread so that fewer people need to seek treatment at any given time is known as "flattening the curve." This helps prevent healthcare systems from becoming overwhelmed. As there is currently no vaccine or specific medication to treat COVID-19 the best way to flatten the curve is through the collective action. Maintaning social distance, wearing mask, self isolation and taking adquate precautions is the best way to prevent the spread and do your part in this time of crisis.
